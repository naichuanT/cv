Mua erases the distance between you.

Many people are in a long-distance relationships. Let's say in the United States, there are more than 14 billion people who think that they are in a long-distance relationship. Although transportation is well developed now, it does not mean that the distance between a pair of lovers is close. A long-distance relationship requires more nutrients and effort to keep it.

We hope that through this app, pairs of lovers in a long-distance relationship can still be closely attached to each other, and move towards a future that both parties yearn for.

Therefore, I use an anonymous google form to give the interviewee a comfortable space without feeling intruded on privacy.

Color theme / Logo

In the design of the whole app, I use the orange of homochromy as the main tone. Homochromy gives people a sense of harmony, coupled with the warmth of orange, which is what we want to bring to users when using it.

I boldly used complementary colors in the logo design. Complementary colors are especially pleasing to the eye because different types of photoreceptor cells, which contribute to color vision, perceive different types of light in the color spectrum. But I made adjustments to the tones of these colors, I hope there can be a little more luxury vibe on it.

In the design of the logo, you can see that it is composed of two circles, these two circles are two bubbles, just like when two people meet, the world of the two people will have many intersections and merge, and the golden line in the outer circle means that the app will help the two people to maintain their own shape while also blending them into each other's world. The gap between the outer circle and the circles is the space the app wants to give the users.

Feature

Couple Calender

Use big pictures and folders, so users can see each milestone at a glance. A year may be long, but when it is divided into small pieces, each day will go faster than we thought.
Swipe left and right to switch between recent events and folders. When users want to add new events, they can see the bullet point of events with the calendar.

Linkshare / Screenshare

There are many ways on this app to help couples have better communication at a distance. For instance, voice recordings and phone calls in the chat room, which can have your tone of voice. The video time can also be matched with facial expressions. But the latter two require both parties to be online at the same time, so we have the function of stickers, using stickers, can better convey emotions, and the different ways of using stickers to answer will also create an exclusive love story only between you.

Chatroom / Video time

Reels uses a scrolling page so that users don't need to jump between apps or go to each app to view inboxes.
Sharing screen can view each other's screen, just like you two are cuddled on the sofa together, your partner is holding a mobile phone to share with you what is interestin.

Future built

Use the game to build the future you dream of together. In this game, there is huge flexibility for construction. The standing out point is that the main income is not from working in the game, but from the interaction time between you and your partner on this app.
There is no inflation in it, so none of your efforts in this relationship will be curtailed. While constructing, you are moving towards this destination together.
